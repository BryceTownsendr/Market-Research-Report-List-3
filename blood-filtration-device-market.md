<p><p>The blood filtration device market includes various types such as hemodialysis membranes, leukocyte filters, and plasma separation membranes. Leukocyte filters are employed during blood transfusions to remove white blood cells and prevent certain complications. Plasma separation membranes are utilized to separate and collect plasma from whole blood, which contains valuable proteins and antibodies. These devices play crucial roles in medical treatments, ensuring the efficient filtration and separation of blood components.</p></p>
