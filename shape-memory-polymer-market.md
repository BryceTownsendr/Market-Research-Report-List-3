<p><p>Shape Memory Polymer market can be categorized into two main types: Heat Induced Type and Electro Induced Type. In the Heat Induced Type market, the shape memory effect is triggered by applying heat to the polymer, causing it to return to its original shape. This type is commonly used in industries such as automotive and aerospace. On the other hand, the Electro Induced Type market involves applying an electric field to the polymer to induce shape memory effects, making it suitable for applications in the medical and electronics industries.</p></p>
