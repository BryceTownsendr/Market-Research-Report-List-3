<p><p>Parallel switchgear is available in two main types: open transition and closed transition. In open transition, the power transfer between two sources occurs with a brief interruption, resulting in a momentary loss of power. On the other hand, closed transition switchgear facilitates a seamless transfer of power between sources, eliminating any interruption. Both types offer advantages based on the specific requirements of the application, allowing for a reliable and efficient power supply solution.</p></p>
