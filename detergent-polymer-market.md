<p><p>Detergent polymer market consists of two main types: liquid polymer and powder polymer. Liquid polymer is a water-soluble polymer used in liquid detergents to enhance their cleaning efficiency and stability. It is easily mixed with other liquid ingredients. Powder polymer, on the other hand, is a solid, dry polymer used in powder detergents to improve their performance in terms of cleaning and fabric care. Both types of polymers play a vital role in the overall effectiveness of detergents in removing stains and dirt from fabrics.</p></p>
