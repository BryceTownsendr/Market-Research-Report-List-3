<p><p>The Asthma Management Products market includes various types of products. Corticosteroids are anti-inflammatory medications that help reduce swelling and mucus production in the airways. Bronchodilators are medications that relax the muscles surrounding the airways, making it easier to breathe. These can be either short-acting or long-acting. Other asthma management products may include leukotriene modifiers, which help prevent certain substances from causing asthma symptoms, and immunomodulators, which help regulate the immune response. These products aim to control asthma symptoms, improve lung function, and prevent asthma attacks.</p></p>
