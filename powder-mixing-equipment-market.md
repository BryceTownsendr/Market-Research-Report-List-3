Double Helix Cone Type Mixer utilizes a rotating cone with helical blades for efficient mixing. Horizontal Weightless Mixer employs a weightless zone to eliminate segregation during blending. Horizontal Plowshare Mixer utilizes plow-shaped blades for thorough mixing. Horizontal Screw Belt Mixer uses a combination of screws and belts to mix powders effectively. Each type offers unique advantages for different mixing applications.</p></p>
