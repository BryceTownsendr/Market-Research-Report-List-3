<p><p>The healthcare wipes market includes two main types: dry wipes and wet wipes. Dry wipes are made from non-woven fabrics and are primarily used for cleaning and wiping surfaces or patients' skin. They are effective in removing dirt and debris without the need for water or other liquid solutions. Wet wipes, on the other hand, are pre-moistened with cleaning or disinfecting solutions. They are commonly used for personal hygiene, wound cleaning, and disinfection purposes in healthcare settings. Both types of wipes play a significant role in maintaining cleanliness and preventing the spread of infections in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
