<p><p>The Enterprise Quality Management Software (EQMS) market can be categorized into three types: cloud-based, web-based, and on-premises. Web-based EQMS is accessible through a web browser, providing similar flexibility as cloud-based solutions. On-premises EQMS, on the other hand, is installed and operated directly on a company's own servers, providing complete control and customization but requiring higher maintenance and IT support.
