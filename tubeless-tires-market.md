<p><p>Tubeless Tires Market can be categorized into two types - Radial Tires and Bias Tires. Radial tires have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better grip and handling characteristics. They are commonly used in passenger cars and light trucks. Bias tires have cords that run at an angle across the tire, providing a smoother ride but less stability. They are often used in off-road vehicles, agricultural equipment, and older vehicles. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on the specific application.</p></p>
