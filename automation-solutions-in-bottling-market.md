<p><p>Automation solutions in the bottling market encompass various technologies. Motion control involves the precise movement of bottles during the bottling process. Filter Regulator Lubricator (Valves and FRLs) control the air supply to machines, ensuring consistent pressure and lubrication. Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) allows for efficient control and monitoring of bottling operations. Distributed Control System (DCS) manages multiple processes and equipment within the bottling line. Manufacturing Execution System (MES) tracks and controls production activities, enabling real-time data collection and analysis for optimal performance.</p></p>
