<p><p>The semiconductor test and measurement service market consists of various types. Front-end engineering testing involves evaluating the electrical performance of semiconductor components during the design phase. Wafer probing tests individual circuits on a semiconductor wafer before it is cut into separate chips. Logic final test examines the functionality of integrated circuits at the end of manufacturing. System-in-package testing focuses on the performance of multiple integrated circuits within a single package. Other types can include reliability testing, failure analysis, and environmental testing, among others. These services ensure the quality and reliability of semiconductor components for various applications.</p></p>
