<p><p>Parasite control market includes various types of products, each designed to combat parasites in different ways. Pills are oral medications taken by animals to remove internal parasites. Injections are administered by veterinarians to provide systemic parasite control. Sprays are applied externally on the animal's body to repel or kill parasites. Ointments are topical treatments applied directly to the affected area for localized parasite control. Additionally, there are other methods available such as collars, shampoos, and spot-on treatments, each offering unique approaches to combat parasites. These diverse options allow pet owners to select the most suitable method for their animal's parasite control needs.</p></p>
