<p><p>Submersible dredge pumps are available in two main types: electric and hydraulic. Electric submersible dredge pumps are powered by electricity and are suitable for smaller scale dredging operations. Hydraulic submersible dredge pumps are powered by hydraulic systems and are preferred for larger scale, heavy-duty dredging projects. Both types of pumps operate underwater to remove sediment, debris, or other materials from bodies of water. The choice between electric and hydraulic submersible dredge pumps depends on the size and complexity of the dredging project.</p></p>
