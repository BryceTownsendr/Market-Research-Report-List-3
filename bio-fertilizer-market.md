<p><p>Bio fertilizers are classified into three main types based on the microorganisms they contain: bacterial fertilizers, actinomycetes fertilizers, and fungi fertilizers. Bacterial fertilizers consist of beneficial bacteria that help in fixing atmospheric nitrogen and solubilizing phosphorus. Actinomycetes fertilizers contain actinomycetes, which play a crucial role in nitrogen fixation and phosphate solubilization. Fungi fertilizers contain beneficial fungi that enhance soil fertility and plant growth. Each type of bio fertilizer has specific benefits and applications in agriculture.</p></p>
