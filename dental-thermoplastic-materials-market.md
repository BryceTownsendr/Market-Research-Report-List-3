<p><p>The Dental Thermoplastic Materials market consists of three major types: transparent, color, and translucent. Transparent thermoplastic materials are clear and allow for visibility of underlying structures. They are commonly used for applications where aesthetics and transparency are important, such as in clear aligners. Color thermoplastic materials provide a wide range of color options, allowing for customization and personalization in dental prosthetics. Translucent thermoplastic materials possess a balanced level of opacity, providing a natural appearance while still allowing some light transmission, which is ideal for dental restorations like crowns and veneers.</p></p>
