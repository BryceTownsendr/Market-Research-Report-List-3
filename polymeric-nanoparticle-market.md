<p><p>Polymeric nanoparticles are a type of nanoparticle composed of various polymers that have unique properties and applications. Two common types in the market include DAB (dendrimer) and PAMAM (polyamidoamine). DAB nanoparticles are highly branched and layered, offering controlled drug delivery and imaging applications. On the other hand, PAMAM nanoparticles are spherical in shape and offer high efficiency in gene delivery and drug delivery due to their branching structure. Both types of polymeric nanoparticles have shown great potential in various biomedical and industrial applications.</p></p>
