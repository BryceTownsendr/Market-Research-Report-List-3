<p><p>The Enteral Nutrition System Market consists of two main types: Enteral Feeding Pumps and Enteral Feeding Tubes. Enteral Feeding Pumps are devices used to deliver liquid nutrition directly into a patient's digestive tract, ensuring proper nutrition intake. These pumps are programmable and provide accurate delivery rates. On the other hand, Enteral Feeding Tubes are thin, flexible tubes inserted through the nose or directly into the stomach to provide nutrition when oral intake is not possible. Both types play a crucial role in supporting patients who cannot consume food or liquids normally.</p></p>
