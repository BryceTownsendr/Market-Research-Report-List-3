<p><strong>The LIB Main Component Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cathode Material</li><li>Anode Material</li><li>Separator</li><li>Electrolyte</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LIB (Lithium-ion battery) main component market consists of several types such as cathode material, anode material, separator, and electrolyte. Cathode material refers to the substance that stores and releases lithium ions during battery charging and discharging. Anode material, on the other hand, acts as the source of these lithium ions. The separator acts as a physical barrier between the cathode and anode, preventing direct contact while allowing the flow of lithium ions. Lastly, the electrolyte is a conductive solution that facilitates the movement of lithium ions between the cathode and anode.</p></p>
