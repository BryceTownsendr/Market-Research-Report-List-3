<p><p>Aircraft defibrillators are available in three main types: automatic, manual, and semi-automatic. Automatic defibrillators analyze the heart rhythm and deliver a shock without the need for user intervention. Manual defibrillators require the user to analyze the heart rhythm and manually deliver a shock. Semi-automatic defibrillators combine elements of both automatic and manual types, requiring the user to analyze the rhythm but automatically delivering the shock. Each type offers varying levels of user control and intervention in emergency situations.</p></p>
