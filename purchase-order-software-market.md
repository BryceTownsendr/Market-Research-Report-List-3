<p><p>Purchase Order Software market is classified into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software refers to the traditional approach of installing the software directly on the company's local servers. This type offers full control and data security, but requires substantial upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet. This type offers a more affordable and flexible solution as it requires no upfront costs and allows for remote access and easy scalability.</p></p>
