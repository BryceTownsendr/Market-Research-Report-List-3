Computed tomography type involves the use of rotating X-ray equipment to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body, allowing for a more precise examination of foreign objects.</p><p>Both types of X-ray foreign detectors are essential tools in medical imaging for accurate diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
