Framboids refer to pyrite formations comprising tiny spherical clusters, often found in sedimentary rocks and used for scientific studies and research. Anastomosing filaments represent intricate pyrite structures with interconnected branches, typically found in rocks formed by hydrothermal processes. These market types cater to different industries and purposes, offering a variety of options for collectors, researchers, and enthusiasts seeking pyrite specimens.</p></p>
