<p><p>The industrial emergency shutdown systems market consists of various types of safety devices. Safety controllers/modules/relays are electronic devices that monitor and control safety functions. Safety switches are used to quickly cut power in an emergency. Logic solver/programmable safety systems use computer programming to perform safety functions. Emergency stop devices allow workers to quickly shut down machinery. Actuators convert energy into motion for safety purposes. Safety sensors detect unsafe conditions. Valves control the flow of materials for safety reasons.</p></p>
