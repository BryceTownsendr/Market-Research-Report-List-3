<p><ul><li>Mechanical Alignment Technique</li><li>Active Optical Assembly Technique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lens centering devices market has two main types: Mechanical Alignment Technique and Active Optical Assembly Technique. Mechanical Alignment Technique involves physically aligning the lens in the center of the optical system using mechanical adjustments. On the other hand, Active Optical Assembly Technique utilizes advanced technologies like sensors and actuators to automatically adjust the position of the lens for precise centering. Both techniques cater to different needs and preferences in the market for ensuring accurate lens alignment in optical systems.</p></p>
