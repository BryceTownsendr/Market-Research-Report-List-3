<p><p>The large size TFT LCD market can be categorized into different types based on the technology used. TN (Twisted Nematic) displays offer fast response times and are cost-effective. IPS (In-Plane Switching) displays provide wider viewing angles and better color accuracy. AFFS (Advanced Fringe Field Switching) panels offer high brightness and low power consumption. MVA (Multi-Domain Vertical Alignment) displays provide improved contrast ratios. Other types may include VA (Vertical Alignment) and OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) displays, each offering unique features and benefits for various applications.</p></p>
