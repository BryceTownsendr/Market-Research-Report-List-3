<p><strong>The Neurological Diagnostic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal</li><li>External</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neurological diagnostic equipment refers to medical devices used to assess and diagnose neurological conditions. The market for this equipment can be categorized into two types: internal and external. Internal equipment refers to devices that are inserted or implanted within the body, such as neurostimulators or intracranial pressure monitors. External equipment, on the other hand, includes devices that are used externally on the body, like electroencephalography (EEG) machines or transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS) devices. Both types serve the purpose of diagnosing neurological disorders but differ in the manner of their usage.</p></p>
