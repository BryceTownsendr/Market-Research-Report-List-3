<p><p>Digital coupons can be classified into two types, namely retailer-generated coupons and manufacturer-generated coupons. Retailer-generated coupons are coupons that are created and distributed by the retailers themselves. These coupons are often specific to a particular retailer and are aimed at attracting customers to their stores or websites. On the other hand, manufacturer-generated coupons are created by product manufacturers and can be used at multiple retailers. These coupons are designed to promote specific products and encourage customers to make a purchase. Both types of digital coupons serve as marketing tools to drive sales and customer engagement.</p></p>
