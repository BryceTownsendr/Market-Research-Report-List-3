<p><ul><li>Visible Light</li><li>Invisible Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Load scanners can be categorized into two main types based on the technology used: Visible Light scanners and Invisible Light scanners. Visible Light scanners utilize cameras and sensors to capture images of the load, providing detailed visual information for analysis. On the other hand, Invisible Light scanners use technologies such as laser or infrared to scan the load without the need for visible light, which can be beneficial for scanning in low light conditions or sensitive materials. Both types of scanners offer unique advantages depending on the specific application.</p></p>
