<p><ul><li>Stochastic Diffusion Search</li><li>Ant Colony Optimization</li><li>Particle Swarm Optimization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swarm Computing refers to a decentralized approach where groups of simple agents cooperate to solve complex problems. Stochastic Diffusion Search divides problem-solving tasks among agents, and they continually exchange and update information until a consensus is reached. Ant Colony Optimization mimics the behavior of ants searching for the shortest path, whereby agents communicate through pheromones to find the optimal solution. Particle Swarm Optimization imitates the behavior of a flock of birds, where agents iteratively adjust their positions based on other agents' fitness, aiming to find the best solution. These techniques enhance problem-solving capabilities through collective intelligence.</p></p>
