<p><strong>The Interior Decorative Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interior decorative coatings market can be classified into two main types based on their composition: waterborne and solvent-borne coatings. Waterborne coatings are made with water as the main solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean up. They are growing in popularity due to their low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). On the other hand, solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents as the main carrier, resulting in a more durable finish but with higher VOC levels. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the interior design industry.</p></p>
