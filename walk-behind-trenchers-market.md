<p><p>Walk-behind trenchers are commonly divided into two main types: wheel trenchers and chain trenchers. Wheel trenchers are equipped with a large, circular wheel that rotates and digs into the ground, making them ideal for softer soil conditions. Chain trenchers, on the other hand, use a continuous digging chain to cut through tougher surfaces like asphalt or concrete. Both types of trenchers are used for various excavation tasks, such as laying utility lines or creating drainage systems.</p></p>
