<p><p>Left Atrial Appendage Closure (LAAC) market includes two types of devices: Endocardial Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) Device and Epicardial LAA Device. The endocardial LAA device is inserted through a catheter and placed inside the LAA, effectively sealing it off and reducing the risk of blood clots forming. On the other hand, the epicardial LAA device is surgically placed on the outside of the heart to achieve the same purpose. These devices aim to lower the risk of stroke in patients with atrial fibrillation by preventing blood clot formation in the LAA.</p></p>
