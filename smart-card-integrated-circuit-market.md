<p><p>Smart Card Integrated Circuit Market includes two main types: Contact and Contactless. Contact smart cards require physical contact with a card reader for data transfer, providing higher security and reliability. Contactless smart cards, on the other hand, use radio frequency identification (RFID) technology for data transfer, allowing for faster and more convenient transactions. Both types are widely used in various industries such as finance, healthcare, and transportation for authentication, payment, and identification purposes.</p></p>
