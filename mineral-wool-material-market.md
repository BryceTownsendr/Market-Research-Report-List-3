<p><p>Mineral wool material is available in various market types, including felty, strip, tubular, granular, and plate. Felty mineral wool is dense and compact, ideal for insulation applications. Strip mineral wool is long and narrow, commonly used for sealing gaps and joints. Tubular mineral wool is cylindrical in shape, suitable for pipe insulation. Granular mineral wool consists of small particles, used for filling cavities. Plate mineral wool is flat and rigid, commonly used for wall and ceiling insulation. Each market type offers unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
