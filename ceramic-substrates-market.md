<p><p>Ceramic substrates are commonly used in electronic applications due to their excellent thermal conductivity and electrical insulation properties. The main types of ceramic substrates in the market are alumina, aluminum nitride, silicon nitride, and beryllium oxide. Alumina substrates are ideal for high power applications, aluminum nitride offers high thermal conductivity, silicon nitride provides exceptional mechanical strength, and beryllium oxide is preferred for its lightweight and high thermal conductivity properties. These substrates cater to various industry demands for effective thermal management and electronic performance.</p></p>
