<p><p>The metal chrome market can be divided into two main types: lump and powder. Lump chrome refers to solid chunks of chrome ore that are typically used for smelting and alloy production. Powder chrome, on the other hand, consists of finely ground chrome ore particles that are commonly used in the production of coatings, pigments, and chemicals. Both lump and powder chrome play important roles in various industries, with each type serving different purposes based on their physical properties and applications.</p></p>
