<p><p>Connected Car Security Solutions Market Types refers to different types of communication systems in the automotive industry. V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure) enables vehicles to communicate with roadside infrastructure to optimize traffic flow and enhance safety. V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) allows direct communication between vehicles to improve road safety and avoid collisions. V2C (Vehicle-to-Cloud) facilitates connectivity between vehicles and cloud-based services, providing real-time updates on traffic, weather, and navigation. V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian) ensures communication between vehicles and pedestrians for increased safety. V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) refers to all types of communication between vehicles, infrastructure, pedestrians, and cloud-based services for a comprehensive connected car ecosystem.</p></p>
