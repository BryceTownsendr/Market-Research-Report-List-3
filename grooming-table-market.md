<p><p>The grooming table market offers various types of tables to cater to different grooming needs. Electric grooming tables are equipped with a motor to adjust their height, making it easy for groomers to handle pets of varying sizes and for pet owners to comfortably place their pets on the table. Hydraulic grooming tables use hydraulic pressure to raise or lower the table to the desired height. Manual grooming tables require physical effort to adjust the table height, usually using a hand crank. The "other" segment encompasses less common types of grooming tables, such as foldable or portable tables that offer convenience and mobility.</p></p>
