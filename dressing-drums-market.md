<p><p>Dressing drums are used in various industries for the process of tumbling or agitating materials. There are two main types of dressing drums in the market. Seamless dressing drums are constructed in one piece, which ensures durability and eliminates the risk of leakage. On the other hand, jointed dressing drums are made with sections that are welded together, making them more cost-effective and easier to transport. Both types serve the same purpose of facilitating the tumbling process, but differ in terms of construction and characteristics.</p></p>
