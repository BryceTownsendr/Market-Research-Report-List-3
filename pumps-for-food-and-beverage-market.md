<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal pumps are commonly used in the food and beverage industry for applications requiring high flow rates and low viscosity fluids. They work by using centrifugal force to move the liquid through the pump. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, are ideal for handling viscous fluids and products with solids or shear-sensitive ingredients. They operate by trapping a specific volume of fluid and pushing it through the system. Both types of pumps are essential for different operations within the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
