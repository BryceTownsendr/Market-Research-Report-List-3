<p><ul><li>Color Masterbatches</li><li>Filler Masterbatches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Additive Masterbatches are categorized into Color Masterbatches and Filler Masterbatches. Color Masterbatches are used to add vibrant colors to plastics, enhancing their aesthetic appeal. They are widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, and consumer goods. On the other hand, Filler Masterbatches are used to improve the physical properties of plastics by adding fillers such as calcium carbonate or talc. They help in reducing costs, enhancing strength, and improving the overall performance of plastic products. Both types of masterbatches play a crucial role in the plastics industry.</p></p>
