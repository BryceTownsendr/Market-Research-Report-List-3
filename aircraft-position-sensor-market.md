<p><p>Linear type position sensors are designed to measure the linear movement of an object, typically along a single axis. They provide accurate and precise position feedback for applications such as aircraft landing gear and throttle control. Angular type position sensors, on the other hand, are used to measure the angular movement of an object, such as the rotation of an aircraft engine or control surfaces. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of aircraft by providing real-time data on the position of various components.</p></p>
