<p><p>FTL (Full Truckload) shipping services refer to shipments that occupy the entire capacity of a truck, typically weighing over 10,000 pounds. FTL provides direct, fast, and dedicated transport while being cost-effective for large-volume shipments. On the other hand, LTL (Less than Truckload) shipping services are used for smaller shipments that do not require the full capacity of a truck. LTL allows multiple shippers to share truck space, resulting in increased efficiency and reduced transportation costs for each individual shipment. Both FTL and LTL offer distinct advantages depending on the size and volume of goods being transported.</p></p>
