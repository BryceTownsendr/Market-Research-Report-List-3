<p><strong>The ZigBee Home Automation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Function Devices (FFDs)</li><li>Low Cost Reduced Function Endpoint Devices (RFDs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ZigBee Home Automation market consists of Full Function Devices (FFDs) and Low Cost Reduced Function Endpoint Devices (RFDs). FFDs are devices that have complete functionality and can act as network coordinators, routers, or end devices. They offer advanced features and can directly interact with users. On the other hand, RFDs are more affordable and provide limited functionality. They are typically end devices without the ability to route or coordinate networks. RFDs are suitable for simple tasks such as turning on/off lights or monitoring sensors.</p></p>
