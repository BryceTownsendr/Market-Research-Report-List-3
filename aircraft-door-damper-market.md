<p><ul><li>Rotary Aircraft Dampers</li><li>Linear Aircraft Dampers</li><li>Axial Aircraft Dampers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft Door Dampers are used to control the opening and closing movement of aircraft doors. The three main types of Aircraft Door Dampers are Rotary Aircraft Dampers, which use a rotary mechanism to control movement, Linear Aircraft Dampers, which operate in a straight line to dampen movement, and Axial Aircraft Dampers, which are designed to absorb force along their axis. Each type of damper serves a specific purpose in regulating the movement of aircraft doors to ensure safety and efficiency during operation.</p></p>
