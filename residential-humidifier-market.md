<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential humidifiers are divided into two main types - Vapor Type Humidifiers and Water Spray Humidifiers. Vapor Type Humidifiers work by boiling water to create steam, which is then released into the air to increase humidity levels. Water Spray Humidifiers, on the other hand, use a rotating disk to break water into small droplets that are then sprayed into the air. Both types of humidifiers are designed to add moisture to the indoor environment, providing relief from dry air and improving comfort levels.</p></p>
