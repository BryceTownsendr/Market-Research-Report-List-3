<p><p>Invoice automation software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet. It offers flexibility, as users can access it from anywhere with an internet connection and updates are managed by the vendor. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed on the user's servers and managed internally. It provides full control over the software and data security, but requires ongoing maintenance and updates by the user's IT department.</p></p>
