<p><ul><li>Belt-Chain Drive CVT</li><li>Planetary CVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt-Chain Drive CVT and Planetary CVT are two types of continuously variable transmission (CVT) gearboxes. In a Belt-Chain Drive CVT system, power is transferred through a belt or chain to smoothly vary the gear ratio. On the other hand, in a Planetary CVT system, power is distributed and controlled through a system of gears and clutches. Both types of CVT gearboxes offer smooth and efficient transmission of power, making them popular choices in various industries such as automotive and industrial machinery.</p></p>
