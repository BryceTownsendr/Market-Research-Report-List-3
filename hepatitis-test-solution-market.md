<p><p>The Hepatitis Test Solution Market offers various types of testing technologies to detect the presence of hepatitis viruses in individuals. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a highly sensitive method that amplifies viral DNA for detection. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) detects antibodies produced by the body in response to the virus. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) provide quick results through detection of viral antigens. Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) is another innovative method for detecting viral RNA. Other technologies also exist for hepatitis testing.</p></p>
