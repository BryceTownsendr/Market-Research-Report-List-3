<p><strong>The High Power Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Stage</li><li>Dual-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Power Isolator Market can be categorized into two types - Single-Stage and Dual-Stage. Single-Stage Isolators provide isolation in a single pass, making them simpler in design and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Dual-Stage Isolators offer higher levels of isolation by passing the signal through two stages, resulting in better performance but also higher complexity and cost. Both types serve the purpose of isolating high power signals effectively, with the choice depending on the specific requirements and trade-offs of the application.</p></p>
