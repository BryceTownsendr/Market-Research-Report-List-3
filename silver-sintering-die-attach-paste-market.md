<p><p>Silver sintering die attach paste market includes two main types: Pressure Sintering and Pressure-less Sintering. Pressure Sintering involves applying pressure during the sintering process to enhance the bond strength between the die and substrate. On the other hand, Pressure-less Sintering does not require external pressure and relies on thermal energy to create strong bonds. Both methods have their own advantages and are widely used in the electronics industry for die attachment in power modules and high-performance applications.</p></p>
