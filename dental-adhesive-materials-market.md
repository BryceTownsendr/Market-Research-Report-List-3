<p><p>Dental adhesive materials are used in dentistry to bond restorative materials, such as crowns, bridges, and veneers, to natural teeth. These materials provide strong and durable adhesion, ensuring stability and longevity of dental restorations. They also help to prevent microleakage, post-operative sensitivity, and secondary caries.</p><p>The market for dental adhesive materials is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of dental diseases, such as tooth decay and periodontal diseases, is a major factor driving the demand for dental adhesive materials. Additionally, the rising demand for esthetic dental procedures and advancements in dental technology are further fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the growing geriatric population, which is more prone to dental problems, is also contributing to the market expansion. Furthermore, the increasing awareness regarding oral health and the availability of advanced dental products and treatments are boosting the adoption of dental adhesive materials.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a shift towards the use of adhesive systems that exhibit improved bond strength, compatibility with various restorative materials, and enhanced aesthetics.
