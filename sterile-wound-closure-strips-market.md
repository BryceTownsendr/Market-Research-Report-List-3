<p><p>Sterile wound closure strips are medical devices used for the closure of minor cuts, lacerations, and surgical incisions. These strips are usually made of non-woven fabric, reinforced with adhesive material, and have low skin irritation properties. They are designed to effectively hold the edges of the wound together and promote healing without the need for stitches or sutures.</p><p>The market for sterile wound closure strips has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. The increasing prevalence of accidents and injuries, particularly in the sports and automotive sectors, has been a key factor contributing to the market growth. Additionally, the rising number of surgical procedures, fueled by advancements in healthcare infrastructure and increased patient awareness, has also boosted market demand.</p><p>Moreover, the convenience offered by these strips in terms of easy application, reduced pain, and scarring as compared to traditional wound closure methods has further enhanced their market adoption. The growing geriatric population worldwide, which is more susceptible to wounds and chronic illnesses, has also been a driving force for market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, there has been a shift towards the development of advanced wound closure strips that offer improved flexibility, breathability, and provide better wound approximation. Additionally, the rising preference for minimally invasive procedures and outpatient surgeries has created a demand for sterile wound closure strips that can be easily applied and removed post-surgery.</p><p>Overall, the sterile wound closure strips market is anticipated to witness robust growth in the coming years.
