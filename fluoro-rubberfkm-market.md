VD FKM offers excellent heat resistance, HFP FKM provides improved low-temperature flexibility, TFE FKM offers enhanced chemical resistance, PMVE FKM has better compression set resistance, and specialty markets cater to specific industry demands. Each type of FKM has unique properties and applications within the market.</p></p>
