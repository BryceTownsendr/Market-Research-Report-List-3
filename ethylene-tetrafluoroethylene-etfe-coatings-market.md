<p><p>Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) coatings can be applied in two main types: powder coating and fluid dipping coating. Powder coating involves applying a dry powder to the surface of the material and then curing it in an oven to create a hard finish. Fluid dipping coating, on the other hand, involves immersing the material in a liquid ETFE solution, allowing it to adhere to the surface, and then curing it to form a durable coating. Both methods offer excellent protection and chemical resistance for various applications.</p></p>
