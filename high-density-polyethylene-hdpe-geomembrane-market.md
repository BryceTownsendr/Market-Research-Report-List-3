<p><p>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Geomembrane market includes three types: Single Layer Geomembranes, Double Layer Geomembranes, and Three Layer Geomembranes. Single Layer Geomembranes are made up of one layer of HDPE material, Double Layer Geomembranes consist of two layers with a protective layer on top, and Three Layer Geomembranes feature an additional core layer for enhanced strength and durability. These different types cater to various needs in applications such as waste containment, water management, and mining.</p></p>
