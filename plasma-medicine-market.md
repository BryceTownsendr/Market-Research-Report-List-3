<p><strong>The Plasma Medicine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitively Coupled Discharges</li><li>Inductively Coupled Discharges</li><li>Plasma Jets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma medicine encompasses various market types, including capacitively coupled discharges, inductively coupled discharges, and plasma jets. Capacitively coupled discharges utilize electrodes to generate a plasma through an electric field, making it suitable for surface treatments and disinfection. Inductively coupled discharges employ electromagnetic fields to ionize gas, enabling efficient plasma generation for medical applications such as wound healing and tissue regeneration. Plasma jets, on the other hand, involve the controlled release of plasma from handheld devices, which can be used for targeted therapies like cancer treatment or precise surgical procedures.</p></p>
