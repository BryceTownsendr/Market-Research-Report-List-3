<p><p>Aluminium sheet market includes two main types: Cold Rolled Aluminium Sheet and Hot Rolled Aluminium Sheet. Cold Rolled Aluminium Sheet is processed at room temperature, resulting in higher strength and smooth surface finish, making it ideal for applications requiring a high level of formability and stability. On the other hand, Hot Rolled Aluminium Sheet is processed at high temperatures, leading to a lower strength but increased ductility, making it suitable for applications where shaping and bending are required. Both types cater to different market segments based on their specific properties and processing methods.</p></p>
