<p><p>The freewheels market consists of various types, including one-way clutches, backstops, clamping elements, locking assemblies, and others. One-way clutches allow rotation in one direction while preventing it in the opposite direction. Backstops ensure that a machine or equipment does not reverse unintentionally. Clamping elements provide secure connections between two rotating components. Locking assemblies firmly couple different shafts together. The "others" category includes different types of freewheels that do not fall into the aforementioned classifications. These diverse freewheel types cater to various industrial applications, enabling efficient and reliable power transmission systems.</p></p>
