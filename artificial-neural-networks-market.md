The second is the Feedback Artificial Neural Network, which allows the flow of information in both directions, enabling feedback loops for improved learning. Lastly, the Others market includes hybrid or specialized variations of ANNs designed for specific purposes, such as recurrent neural networks or convolutional neural networks.</p></p>
