<p><p>Bio-Dispersing Agents are categorized into different types based on their chemical properties and functionality. The Anionic Type of dispersing agents have a negative charge, allowing them to repel each other and disperse evenly in a solution. The Cationic Type, on the other hand, have a positive charge and work well in dispersing particles in polar solvents. Other types of dispersing agents include non-ionic and amphoteric agents, which have varying properties and applications in different industries. Each type of dispersing agent offers unique benefits and is chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
