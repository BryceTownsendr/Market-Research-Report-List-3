<p><p>Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) are widely used for bulk transportation of goods. There are four main types of FIBCs based on their level of electrostatic protection: Type A, Type B, Type C, and Type D. Type A FIBCs are standard bags without any special safety features. Type B FIBCs are made from non-conductive fabrics but do not offer full protection against static electricity. Type C FIBCs have conductive threads to dissipate static electricity. Type D FIBCs are the safest as they are constructed from anti-static materials to prevent the occurrence of incendiary discharges.</p></p>
