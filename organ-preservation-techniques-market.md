<p><p>Organ preservation techniques are used to maintain the quality and viability of organs for transplantation. The static cold storage method involves preserving organs in a cooled solution to slow down metabolism. Organ preservation fluids are specialized solutions that help maintain organ function during transportation and storage. Machine perfusion techniques involve using a perfusion machine to pump oxygenated blood or preservation fluid into the organ, mimicking its natural environment and extending preservation time. These techniques play a crucial role in preventing organ damage and increasing the success rate of transplantation surgeries.</p></p>
