<p><ul><li>Single-Level Ventilator</li><li>Bi-Level Ventilator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-invasive home ventilator market includes various types of devices. Single-level ventilators deliver a constant pressure throughout the breathing cycle, making it suitable for patients with stable breathing conditions. Bi-level ventilators provide a higher pressure during inhalation and a lower pressure during exhalation, helping patients with fluctuating respiratory needs. Other types may include volume ventilators, which deliver a specific volume of air, and pressure support ventilators, which assist patients with inspiratory efforts. These different types cater to different respiratory conditions and provide varying levels of support at home.</p></p>
