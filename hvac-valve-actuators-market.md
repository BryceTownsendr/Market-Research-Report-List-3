<p><p>The HVAC Valve Actuators market encompasses various types such as floating, modulating, pneumatic, proportional, and others. Floating actuators control the valve by allowing it to fully open or close. Modulating actuators adjust the position of the valve, ensuring precise control of the flow. Pneumatic actuators employ compressed air to operate the valve. Proportional actuators regulate the valve position based on a control signal. Lastly, the market also includes other types of valve actuators that may have unique functionalities or features, providing further options for HVAC system control.</p></p>
