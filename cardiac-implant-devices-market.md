<p><p>The cardiac implant devices market includes various types of devices used in the treatment of heart-related conditions. Implantable cardiac pacemakers (ICPs) are devices that regulate the heart's electrical signals to maintain a normal heartbeat. Implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs) monitor heart rhythm and deliver electric shocks to restore normal rhythm in case of irregularities. Cardiac resynchronization therapy devices (CRTs) are used to coordinate the heart's pumping action. Coronary stents are small metal tubes that are inserted into blocked arteries to improve blood flow. Other devices in this market include heart valves and ventricular assist devices.</p></p>
