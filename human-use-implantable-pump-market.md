<p><p>A human use implantable pump is a medical device used to deliver medication or hormones directly into the body. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases such as cancer and cardiovascular disorders is driving the demand for implantable pumps. These devices provide a more effective and efficient method of drug delivery compared to traditional oral medications. Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of smarter and more sophisticated implantable pumps, further driving the market growth.</p><p>Another key trend in the human use implantable pump market is the growing adoption of wireless technology. Wireless pumps allow healthcare providers to remotely monitor and control the dosage, making it easier to adjust the medication as per the patient's changing needs. Moreover, these devices also provide real-time data, allowing healthcare professionals to make informed decisions regarding the treatment.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing an increasing number of strategic collaborations and partnerships between pharmaceutical companies, medical device manufacturers, and healthcare providers.
