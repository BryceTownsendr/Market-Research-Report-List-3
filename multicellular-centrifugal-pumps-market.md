<p><strong>The Multicellular Centrifugal Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Multicellular Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Vertical Multicellular Centrifugal Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multicellular centrifugal pumps are classified into two main types based on the orientation of the pump's shaft. Horizontal multicellular centrifugal pumps have a horizontal shaft, making them suitable for applications where space is limited or when the pump needs to be installed above the liquid level. On the other hand, vertical multicellular centrifugal pumps have a vertical shaft, making them ideal for applications where the pump needs to be submerged in the liquid being pumped or when the pump needs to lift liquid from a lower level.</p></p>
