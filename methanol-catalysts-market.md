<p><p>Methanol Catalysts Market Types include Methanol Synthesis and Methanol Reforming. Methanol Synthesis involves the production of methanol from synthesis gas (a mixture of hydrogen and carbon monoxide) using catalysts such as copper-based catalysts. Methanol Reforming, on the other hand, involves converting methanol into hydrogen and carbon dioxide using catalysts like nickel-based catalysts. Both processes play a crucial role in the production and conversion of methanol for various industrial applications.</p></p>
