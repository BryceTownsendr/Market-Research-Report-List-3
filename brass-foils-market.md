<p><p>Brass foils market types include Oxygen-Free High Conductivity Copper (OFHC), Electrolytic Tough Pitch (ETP) Copper, Deoxidized High Phosphorous (DHP) Copper, and Deoxidized Low Phosphorous (DLP) Copper. OFHC copper is known for its high conductivity and oxygen-free properties, while ETP copper is valued for its toughness and pitch quality. DHP copper contains high phosphorous for improved corrosion resistance, while DLP copper has low phosphorous content for better cold working capabilities. Each type caters to specific industrial applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
