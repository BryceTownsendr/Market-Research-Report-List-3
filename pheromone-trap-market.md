<p><p>Pheromone traps are categorized into different types based on their design and shape. The most common types include bottle traps, funnel traps, and delta traps. Bottle traps are designed to attract and trap insects inside a small container, while funnel traps have a funnel-shaped entrance that leads insects into a collection chamber. Delta traps are triangular in shape and are effective for monitoring pests in agricultural settings.
