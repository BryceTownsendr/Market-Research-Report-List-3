<p><p>Nephro Genetic Testing Market includes Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) and Multiplex Ligation-dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA) which are advanced genetic testing technologies used for detecting genetic mutations and variations associated with kidney diseases. NGS enables simultaneous sequencing of multiple genes in a single run, providing comprehensive genetic information for accurate diagnosis. On the other hand, MLPA is a targeted gene analysis technique that identifies specific genetic abnormalities in a sample. Both technologies play a crucial role in personalized medicine and improving patient care in nephrology.</p></p>
