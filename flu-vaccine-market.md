<p><p>Flu vaccine, also known as influenza vaccine, is a preventive measure used to protect against the infection of influenza viruses. It stimulates the immune system to develop antibodies that recognize and target specific strains of the virus, thereby reducing the risk of flu-related complications and hospitalization.</p><p>The global flu vaccine market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing prevalence of flu infections worldwide. The market growth can be attributed to several factors, such as the growing awareness about the benefits of vaccination, government initiatives to control flu outbreaks, and the introduction of innovative vaccine technologies.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the flu vaccine market is the development of quadrivalent vaccines. Quadrivalent vaccines provide protection against four different strains of the flu virus, including two influenza A strains and two influenza B strains. This advancement ensures broader coverage and higher efficacy compared to traditional trivalent vaccines. Moreover, the use of cell-based and recombinant technologies for vaccine production has gained traction, leading to improved vaccine effectiveness.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a rising demand for needle-free and nasal spray vaccines. These alternative administration methods are gaining popularity due to their convenience, especially for children and individuals with a fear of needles.</p><p>Another significant trend is the increasing adoption of flu vaccines by various age groups.
