<p><strong>The Automotive TPMS (Tire-Pressure Monitoring System) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive TPMS market is divided into two types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors to measure the tire pressure in real time and provides accurate information to the driver. Indirect TPMS, on the other hand, calculates tire pressure based on other vehicle parameters like wheel speed and ABS data. Direct TPMS is more precise and reliable but also more expensive, while Indirect TPMS is generally cheaper but may not be as accurate in measuring tire pressure.</p></p>
