<p><p>Roughness and contour measuring machines are essential tools used in various industries to measure the surface texture and shape of a component. There are two main types of machines in the market: contact and non-contact. Contact machines utilize a stylus or probe to physically touch the surface and provide measurements, while non-contact machines use optical or laser technology to measure without touching the surface. Both types offer high precision and accuracy in capturing roughness and contour data for quality control and inspection purposes.</p></p>
