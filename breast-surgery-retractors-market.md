<p><p>Breast surgery retractors are medical instruments used to facilitate access and provide clear visibility during breast surgeries. They come in two types: single-arm retractors and double-arm retractors. Single-arm retractors consist of one arm that holds the incision open, allowing the surgeon to access the surgical area. On the other hand, double-arm retractors have two opposing arms that hold the incision open, providing a larger working space for the surgeon. Both types of retractors play a crucial role in breast surgery by maintaining proper exposure and ensuring successful procedures.</p></p>
