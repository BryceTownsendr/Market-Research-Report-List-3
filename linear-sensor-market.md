<p><ul><li>Capacitance Sensors</li><li>Eddy Current Sensors</li><li>Photoelectric Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear sensors are classified into various types based on their sensing mechanism. Capacitance sensors measure changes in capacitance between two plates. Eddy current sensors detect changes in eddy currents induced in a conductive target. Photoelectric sensors use light to detect objects. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to measure distance. Other types of linear sensors include inductive sensors, resistive sensors, and magnetic sensors. Each type has unique features and applications, making them suitable for different industries and environments.</p></p>
