<p><p>A fluidized bed dryer is a type of industrial dryer that utilizes the principle of fluidization to efficiently dry a wide range of materials. In this dryer, a bed of solid particles is suspended and agitated by an upward flow of gas. This creates a fluid-like state where the solids behave like a fluid, allowing for better contact between the material and the drying medium. The fluidization process enhances heat and mass transfer, resulting in faster and more uniform drying.</p><p>The global fluidized bed dryer market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Fluidized Bed Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibrated Fluid Bed</li><li>Fluid-bed Granulation</li><li>Spouted Bed Dryer</li><li>Mechanically Agitated Fluid-bed Dryer</li><li>Centrifugal Fluid-bed Dryer</li><li>Fluidized Spray Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluidized bed dryer market includes various types such as vibrated fluid bed, fluid-bed granulation, spouted bed dryer, mechanically agitated fluid-bed dryer, centrifugal fluid-bed dryer, and fluidized spray dryer. Vibrated fluid bed dryers use vibration to enhance the fluidization of particles. Fluid-bed granulation involves the formation of granules through the spraying of liquid onto fluidized particles. Spouted bed dryers utilize spouting gas for effective heat and mass transfer. Mechanically agitated fluid-bed dryers have a mechanical agitation system to enhance drying. Centrifugal fluid-bed dryers use centrifugal force to fluidize particles. Fluidized spray dryers involve atomizing a liquid feed into droplets and drying them in a fluidized bed.</p></p>
