<p><ul><li>Flatbed Agitator</li><li>Circular Agitator</li><li>Combination Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platelet agitators are medical devices used to store blood platelets at a controlled temperature and agitation to prevent clumping and maintain viability. There are three main types of platelet agitators on the market: flatbed agitators, circular agitators, and combination devices. Flatbed agitators have a large surface area for platelet storage, circular agitators rotate the platelets in a circular motion, and combination devices offer both flatbed and circular agitation options. Each type has its own unique benefits and features for efficiently storing platelets.</p></p>
