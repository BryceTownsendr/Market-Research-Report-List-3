<p><p>The pneumatic control component market is divided into three main types: directional control valves, pressure control valves, and flow control valves. Directional control valves are used to control the direction of air flow in an pneumatic system. Pressure control valves regulate the pressure of the air flow within the system. Flow control valves manage the rate at which the air flows through the system. Each type of valve plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and effective operation of pneumatic systems.</p></p>
