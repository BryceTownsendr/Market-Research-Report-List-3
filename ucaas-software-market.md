<p><p>UCaaS software market includes two types - on-premise and cloud-based solutions. </p><p>On-premise solutions are installed and hosted within an organization's own infrastructure, providing full control and customization. This requires dedicated hardware, software licenses, and maintenance by the organization's IT team.</p><p>Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and reduced upfront costs as organizations can pay for services on a subscription basis. They are managed and maintained by the service provider, allowing businesses to focus on their core activities.</p></p>
