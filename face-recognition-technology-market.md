<p><p>Face recognition technology is a rapidly growing market that offers different types of solutions. </p><p>1) 2D Facial Recognition relies on analyzing a two-dimensional image of a person's face. This type of recognition is useful for non-contact temperature screening, particularly during times of public health emergencies.</p></p>
