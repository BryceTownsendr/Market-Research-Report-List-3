<p><strong>The Electrodialysis Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Electrodialysis</li><li>Batch Electrodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrodialysis equipment market can be classified into two main types: continuous electrodialysis and batch electrodialysis. Continuous electrodialysis involves the continuous separation of ions from a feed solution using a membrane stack, allowing for a continuous flow of product and waste stream. On the other hand, batch electrodialysis operates in a batch mode, where the feed solution is separated in batches, resulting in intermittent product and waste flow. These differing types cater to different industry needs and have varying advantages and limitations in terms of operational efficiency and production scale.</p></p>
