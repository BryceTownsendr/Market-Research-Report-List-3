<p><p>Dry ice blasting and cleaning equipment is a highly efficient and environmentally friendly method of removing unwanted substances from various surfaces. It involves the use of small, high-velocity pellets of dry ice that are blasted onto the surface, instantly freezing and lifting the contaminants, such as paint, grease, oil, or mold. The dry ice pellets sublimate, meaning they convert from a solid to a gas, leaving behind no residue or waste.
