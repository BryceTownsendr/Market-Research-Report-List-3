<p><p>Glass balustrade systems are commonly categorized into two main types: Structural Glass Balustrades and Frameless Glass Balustrades. Structural glass balustrades are characterized by the use of thick, tempered glass panels that are supported by metal or wooden posts for added stability. On the other hand, Frameless glass balustrades feature seamless sheets of glass without any visible posts or frames, creating a modern and sleek aesthetic. Both types of balustrade systems provide safety and style to spaces such as staircases, balconies, and terraces.</p></p>
