<p><p>PVDF Binder for Battery Market Types are distinguished based on the polymerization process: Emulsion Polymerization and Suspension Polymerization. Emulsion Polymerization involves mixing PVDF monomers with water and surfactants to create a stable emulsion, while Suspension Polymerization involves dispersing PVDF monomers in a solvent with a suspending agent. Both processes result in the formation of PVDF binders with different properties, making them suitable for specific applications in the battery industry. Both types offer unique advantages and are widely used in the market.</p></p>
