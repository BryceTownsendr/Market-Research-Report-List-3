<p><p>Pharmaceutical lactose market types refer to different forms of lactose used in the pharmaceutical industry. Crystalline monohydrate lactose is a highly pure and crystalline form used primarily in the production of tablets and capsules. Inhalation lactose is specifically designed for use in inhalation drug delivery systems, ensuring effective drug delivery to the respiratory system. Granulated lactose is a powdered form used in the development of oral solid dosage forms, providing good flowability and compressibility. Spray-dried lactose is produced by drying a lactose solution using a spray nozzle, resulting in a fine powder suitable for various applications, including dry powder inhalers.</p></p>
