<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) Pipes are widely used in various industries due to their durability and corrosion resistance. There are two main types of FRP pipes: Glass FRP Pipes and Carbon FRP Pipes. Glass FRP Pipes are made from glass fibers and are known for their lightweight and low cost, making them suitable for applications such as water treatment and sewage systems. Carbon FRP Pipes, on the other hand, are reinforced with carbon fibers, providing high strength and chemical resistance, making them ideal for harsh environments like chemical processing plants and oil refineries.</p></p>
