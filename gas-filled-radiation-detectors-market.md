<p><ul><li>Ionization Chamber</li><li>Proportional Counter</li><li>Geiger-Müller Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas-filled radiation detectors are devices used to detect and measure ionizing radiation. The three main types of gas-filled radiation detectors are ionization chambers, proportional counters, and Geiger-Müller counters. Ionization chambers are most commonly used for measuring high levels of radiation, proportional counters are more sensitive and used for medium levels of radiation, and Geiger-Müller counters are the most sensitive and commonly used for detecting low levels of radiation. Each type of detector has specific applications and advantages depending on the level of radiation being measured.</p></p>
