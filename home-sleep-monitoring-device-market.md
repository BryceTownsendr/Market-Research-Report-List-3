<p><p>A Home Sleep Monitoring Device is a portable device used to record various physiological parameters during sleep, such as breathing patterns, heart rate, oxygen levels, and movements. One of the key drivers of this market is the rising prevalence of sleep disorders across the globe. Factors such as stress, lifestyle changes, and increasing geriatric population are contributing to the increasing prevalence of sleep disorders, thereby increasing the demand for home sleep monitoring devices.</p><p>Moreover, the advancements in technology have led to the development of more accurate and user-friendly home sleep monitoring devices. The integration of wireless technology into these devices enables easy monitoring and data transfer, making it convenient for both patients and healthcare professionals.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has also positively impacted the market growth. <p><p>The home sleep monitoring device market can be divided into two types: wearable devices and non-wearable devices.
