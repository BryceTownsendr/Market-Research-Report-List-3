<p><p>Aluminum flat wire is commonly used in the electrical market in two main types: paper-wrapped and electrical. This type is commonly used in transformer windings and other electrical applications where insulation is crucial. Both types serve key roles in the electrical market by providing the necessary conductivity and insulation for various electronic applications.</p></p>
