<p><p>The Free Thyroxine Detection Kit market consists of various types of samples such as serum, hep plasma, and cit plasma. Serum samples are obtained by separating blood cells from the blood, and are widely used in clinical settings for diagnosing thyroid disorders. Hep plasma samples are obtained by anticoagulating blood with heparin, while cit plasma samples are obtained by anticoagulating blood with sodium citrate. These plasma samples have different uses, with hep plasma being commonly used in research studies and cit plasma being used in coagulation testing.</p></p>
