<p><ul><li>Low Level Waste</li><li>Intermediate Level Waste</li><li>High Level Waste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear waste management systems are categorized into three main types based on the level of radioactivity: low level waste, intermediate level waste, and high level waste. Low level waste includes items like protective clothing and tools that have been contaminated with radioactive materials, while intermediate level waste consists of materials with higher levels of radioactivity, such as reactor components. High level waste is the most dangerous type, with highly radioactive materials like spent nuclear fuel. Each type of waste requires specialized handling and disposal methods to ensure safety and environmental protection.</p></p>
