<p><p>Pharmaceutical packaging and filling machines are divided into three main types. Primary packaging machines are responsible for directly containing the drugs or medications in various forms such as bottles, tubes, blister packs, or vials. Secondary packaging machines handle the packaging of primary packaging units into larger units like cartons or bundles. Lastly, the "Others" market category includes specialized machines used for labeling, capping, wrapping, or sealing pharmaceutical products. These machines ensure the accurate and efficient packaging and filling of pharmaceutical substances, contributing to the safety and convenience of both manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
