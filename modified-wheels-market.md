<p><p>In the Modified Wheels market, three main types of forging are common: One Piece Forging, Two-Piece Forging, and Three Piece Forging. One Piece Forging involves shaping a single piece of metal into the wheel design. Two-Piece Forging involves joining two separate pieces together to create the wheel. Three Piece Forging involves three separate pieces – the rim, center disc, and outer barrel – being joined together to form the wheel. Each type offers unique advantages and customization options for customers.</p></p>
