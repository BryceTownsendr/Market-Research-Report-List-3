<p><ul><li>Worm Gear</li><li>Bevel Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Valve gear boxes come in different types, including Worm Gear and Bevel Gear. Worm Gear boxes utilize a screw-like mechanism to transmit motion between two non-intersecting shafts, providing smooth and efficient power transmission. On the other hand, Bevel Gear boxes use cone-shaped gears to transfer motion between intersecting shafts at various angles. Both types of gear boxes are commonly used in various industrial applications where precise and reliable power transmission is crucial.</p></p>
