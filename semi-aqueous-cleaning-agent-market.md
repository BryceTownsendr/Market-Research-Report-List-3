<p><p>Semi-Aqueous Cleaning Agents are divided into two main types: Water-Soluble Solvent Type and Water-Insoluble Solvent Type. The Water-Soluble Solvent Type contains solvents that can be easily dissolved in water, making them ideal for removing water-soluble contaminants. On the other hand, Water-Insoluble Solvent Type contains solvents that do not mix well with water, making them effective for removing oil-based contaminants. Both types offer unique cleaning properties and are suitable for different industrial applications.</p></p>
