<p><p>Eddy current testing solutions are used for non-destructive testing to detect cracks, defects, and conductive material thickness. The feed-through coil market includes solutions that use a coil that passes through the tested object, providing quick and efficient measurements. Probe coil solutions involve a handheld probe that is moved over the surface of the material to detect any flaws. Interpolation coil solutions use multiple coils placed in a specific pattern to provide precise measurements and imaging capabilities. These market types offer different approaches to eddy current testing depending on the requirements and limitations of the testing scenario.</p></p>
