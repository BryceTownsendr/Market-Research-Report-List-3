On the other hand, circuit encapsulation adhesives are designed to encapsulate the entire circuit board, providing a protective coating that enhances durability and reliability. These adhesives come in various forms such as liquid encapsulants, potting compounds, and conformal coatings, catering to different types of circuit board protection needs.</p></p>
