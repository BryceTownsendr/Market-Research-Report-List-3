<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Delivery Systems</li><li>Common Rail Delivery Systems</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive fuel delivery systems include Pump-Line-Nozzle delivery systems, Common Rail delivery systems, and other emerging technologies. Pump-Line-Nozzle systems are traditional and use a mechanical pump to deliver fuel to the injectors. Common Rail systems are more advanced and use a high-pressure pump to maintain constant pressure in the fuel rail. Other systems may include direct injection, integrated fuel modules, or hybrid systems. Each type has its own benefits and drawbacks, catering to different vehicle specifications and performance requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
