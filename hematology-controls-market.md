<p><p>Hematology controls refer to substances used to calibrate and monitor the accuracy of hematology analyzers or blood testing systems. There are different types of hematology controls available in the market. 3-Part Hematology Controls are designed for analyzers that classify blood cells into three categories (lymphocytes, monocytes, and granulocytes). 5-Part Hematology Controls are used with analyzers that classify blood cells into five categories (lymphocytes, monocytes, neutrophils, eosinophils, and basophils). Other types of hematology controls are designed for specific analyzers or offer unique features depending on the requirements of a laboratory or diagnostic center.</p></p>
