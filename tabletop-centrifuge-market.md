<p><p>Tabletop centrifuge market can be categorized into two main types based on their operational speed: Low Speed and High Speed centrifuges. Low Speed centrifuges operate at lower speeds, typically below 10,000 rpm, and are ideal for gentle sedimentation of biological substances. On the other hand, High Speed centrifuges operate at higher speeds, above 10,000 rpm, and are used for rapid separation of components in samples. Each type offers distinct advantages and is tailored for specific applications in various industries such as healthcare, research, and biotechnology.</p></p>
