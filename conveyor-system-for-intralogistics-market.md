<p><p>The conveyor system for intralogistics market encompasses various types of conveyors used for material handling purposes within a facility. Roller conveyors are designed with rotating rollers to transport goods smoothly. Belt conveyors consist of a belt stretched between two pulleys to transport items. Overhead conveyors are suspended above ground, allowing easy movement of goods. Pallet conveyors are specifically designed for palletized goods. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various types such as slat conveyors, chain conveyors, and screw conveyors, which are used for specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
