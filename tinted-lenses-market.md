<p><strong>The Tinted Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Tint Lenses</li><li>Gradient Tint Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tinted lenses are commonly categorized into two main types: fixed tint lenses and gradient tint lenses. Fixed tint lenses have a consistent tint throughout the entire lens, providing uniform protection from sunlight. On the other hand, gradient tint lenses have a gradual shift in tint, being darker at the top and lighter at the bottom. This design allows for better visibility in various lighting conditions, making them a popular choice for sunglasses. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market for tinted lenses.</p></p>
