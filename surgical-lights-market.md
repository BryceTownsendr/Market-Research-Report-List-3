<p><p>Surgical lights are essential equipment used in operating rooms to provide adequate lighting during surgical procedures. There are two main types of surgical lights: LED lights and halogen lights. LED lights utilize light-emitting diodes as a source of illumination, offering bright and energy-efficient lighting. Halogen lights, on the other hand, make use of halogen gas to produce intense white light. Both types of lights are designed to provide surgeons with a clear view of the surgical field, ensuring optimal visibility and accuracy during procedures.</p></p>
