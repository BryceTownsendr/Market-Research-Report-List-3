<p><p>Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) Film is a technology used in smart windows and glazing applications. PDLC film consists of liquid crystal droplets dispersed in a polymer matrix, which changes its light scattering properties in response to an electrical voltage. This allows the film to switch between opaque and transparent states, providing privacy, glare reduction, and energy efficiency benefits.</p><p>The market for PDLC film is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period. <p><p>Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) Film is available in two types based on adhesive properties. None-Adhesive PDLC Film does not have any adhesive backing and requires an external adhesive for installation. On the other hand, Self-Adhesive PDLC Film comes with a built-in adhesive layer, making it easy to install on various surfaces without the need for additional adhesive. Both types offer the versatility of switchable transparency and privacy control, making them suitable for applications in architecture, automotive, and smart devices.</p></p>
