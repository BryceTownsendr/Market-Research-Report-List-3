<p><p>An EKG sensor, also known as an electrocardiogram sensor, is a device used to measure the electrical activity of the heart. Manufacturers are focusing on developing portable and wireless EKG sensors, making them more convenient for patients and healthcare providers.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing geriatric population and the rise in lifestyle-related risk factors, such as obesity and sedentary lifestyles, are contributing to the market growth. These factors increase the demand for cardiac monitoring, leading to an increased adoption of EKG sensors.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards remote monitoring solutions.
