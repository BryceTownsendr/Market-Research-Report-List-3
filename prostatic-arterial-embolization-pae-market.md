<p><p>Prostatic Arterial Embolization (PAE) is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), a condition characterized by the enlargement of the prostate gland. PAE involves blocking the blood flow to the prostate by injecting tiny particles into the arteries supplying it, leading to the shrinkage of the gland and alleviation of urinary symptoms.</p><p>The Prostatic Arterial Embolization (PAE) Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by several factors. Firstly, the rising prevalence of BPH among aging populations is fueling the demand for minimally invasive treatment options such as PAE.
