<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Mold Steel</li><li>Cold Rolled Mold Steel</li><li>Plastic Mold Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Special Steel for mold market comprises three main types: Hot Rolled Mold Steel, Cold Rolled Mold Steel, and Plastic Mold Steel. Hot Rolled Mold Steel is produced by heating the steel material and then rolling it into the desired shape. Cold Rolled Mold Steel is manufactured by cooling the heated steel material and rolling it into shape. Plastic Mold Steel is specially designed for use in molding plastic materials, offering high durability and resistance to wear and tear. These three types cater to different needs in the mold-making industry.</p></p>
