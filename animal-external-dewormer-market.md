<p><p>Animal external dewormers are products used to treat and prevent parasitic infestations in animals. There are two main types available in the market: injection and unguent. Injection dewormers are administered through a needle and directly into the animal's bloodstream, providing quick and effective treatment. Unguent dewormers, on the other hand, are topical products applied externally to the animal's skin or fur. Both types are commonly used by pet owners and livestock farmers to keep their animals healthy and free from parasites.</p></p>
