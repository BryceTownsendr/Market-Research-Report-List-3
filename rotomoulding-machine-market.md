<p><p>Rotomoulding machines are used to manufacture hollow plastic products by heating and rotating molds. The market is segmented into three types of machines: Shuttle Rotomoulding Machine, Carrousel Rotomoulding Machine, and Rocking Oven Rotomoulding Machine. Shuttle machines have a single arm that moves in and out of the heating chamber, while carrousel machines have multiple arms that rotate around a central axis. Rocking oven machines have a fixed mold that rocks back and forth during the heating process. Each type of machine offers unique advantages for different manufacturing needs.</p></p>
