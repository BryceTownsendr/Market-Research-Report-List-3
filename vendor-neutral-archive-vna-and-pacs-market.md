VNA allows healthcare providers to access and share patient data seamlessly across different systems and healthcare facilities.</p><p>PACS, on the other hand, is a specific system used for the storage, retrieval, and distribution of medical images such as X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans. It provides healthcare professionals with the ability to view and diagnose medical images digitally instead of using traditional film-based methods.</p><p>The Vendor Neutral Archive (VNA) and PACS Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors.
