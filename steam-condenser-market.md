<p><p>Steam condensers are devices used to condense steam into liquid form for reuse in various industries. There are two main types of steam condensers: Jet condensers, which use water jets to convert steam into liquid, and Surface condensers, which rely on a series of tubes or coils to achieve the same result. While jet condensers are simpler in design and less expensive, surface condensers are more efficient and widely used in industries where large quantities of steam need to be condensed.</p></p>
