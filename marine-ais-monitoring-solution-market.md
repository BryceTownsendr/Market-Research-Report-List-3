<p><p>Marine AIS (Automatic Identification System) monitoring solution is a technology that allows for the real-time tracking and monitoring of vessels in the marine environment. This information is collected and processed by a network of AIS base stations or satellites, enabling marine authorities and stakeholders to have a comprehensive understanding of vessel movements and activities.</p><p>The marine AIS monitoring solution market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period. These onshore systems receive and analyze AIS data to manage vessel traffic and ensure maritime safety. (2) Vessel Based Market involves the installation of AIS monitoring solutions directly on vessels. These systems enable ship owners and operators to track their own vessels' movements and receive real-time information about nearby vessels for navigation and collision avoidance purposes.</p></p>
