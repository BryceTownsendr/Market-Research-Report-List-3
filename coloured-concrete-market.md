<p><p>Coloured concrete can be classified into two main types: Synthetic and Natural. </p><p>Synthetic coloured concrete is created by adding pigments or dyes to the concrete mix during the mixing process. This type of coloured concrete offers a wide range of vibrant and consistent colour options.</p><p>On the other hand, Natural coloured concrete is achieved through the use of natural materials such as mineral oxides, iron oxide pigments, and other substances. This type of coloured concrete tends to offer more earthy and muted tones that blend well with natural surroundings.</p></p>
