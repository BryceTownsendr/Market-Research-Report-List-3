<p><p>Automotive exhaust manifold market types include single wall and double wall designs. Single wall exhaust manifolds consist of a single layer of material, making them lightweight and cost-effective. They are commonly used in standard vehicles. On the other hand, double wall exhaust manifolds have an added layer for improved heat insulation and durability. They are preferred in high-performance vehicles and heavy-duty applications where the exhaust temperatures are higher. Each type caters to different needs based on performance requirements and vehicle types.</p></p>
