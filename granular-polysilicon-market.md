<p><strong>The Granular Polysilicon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SilaneMethod</li><li>MetallurgicalMethod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Granular Polysilicon market is divided into two main types: Silane Method and Metallurgical Method. Silane Method involves the deposition of polysilicon from silane gas in a chemical vapor deposition process, resulting in high-quality granular polysilicon ideal for use in the electronics industry. In contrast, the Metallurgical Method involves the reduction of silicon tetrachloride with high-purity silicon metal, producing lower-cost granular polysilicon suitable for solar panel production and other applications requiring less stringent purity requirements.</p></p>
