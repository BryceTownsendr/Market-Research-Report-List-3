<p><p>The Visual Effects Services market is divided into two main categories: Special Effects and Digital Effects. Special Effects involve the creation of visual illusions using physical props, pyrotechnics, or mechanical devices during film production. These effects are achieved on-set and include activities like explosions, stunts, or animatronics. On the other hand, Digital Effects refer to the manipulation and creation of visual elements using computer software. These effects are added in post-production and include activities like CGI (Computer-Generated Imagery), green screen compositing, or 3D animation. Both Special Effects and Digital Effects contribute to enhancing the visual storytelling in films and other media.</p></p>
