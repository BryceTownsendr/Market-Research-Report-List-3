<p><strong>The Aqueous Polyurethane Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion</li><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aqueous Polyurethane Resin refers to a type of polyurethane resin that is dispersed or emulsified in water. Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion is a type of resin where the polyurethane is dispersed in water to form a stable solution, typically used in coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion is a type of resin where the polyurethane is emulsified in water to form a stable mixture, commonly used in coatings for wood, metal, and plastic surfaces. Both market types offer environmentally-friendly alternatives to traditional solvent-based polyurethane resins.</p></p>
