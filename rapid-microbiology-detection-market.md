<p><p>Rapid microbiology detection markets can be categorized into different types based on the method of detection. Growth-based methods rely on the increase in microbial biomass, while viability-based methods assess the metabolic activity of the microorganism. Cellular component-based methods detect specific components of the microbial cell. Nucleic acid-based methods target the genetic material of the microorganism. Other types of detection methods include immunological assays and biochemical tests. Each type offers advantages and limitations in terms of sensitivity, specificity, and speed of detection.</p></p>
