<p><p>There are two main types of dartboards in the market: Bristle Dartboards and Electric Dartboards. Bristle dartboards are made from sisal fibers, allowing the darts to stick in the board tightly and reducing bounce-outs. They are popular among professional players for their durability and authenticity. On the other hand, Electric Dartboards use sensors to track scores automatically and have built-in games for added entertainment. They are suitable for casual players and families looking for a fun and interactive dart-playing experience.</p></p>
