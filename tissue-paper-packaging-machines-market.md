<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tissue paper packaging machines are available in two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines operate without human intervention, performing all functions from packaging to sealing autonomously. They are suitable for high-volume production and offer enhanced efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual involvement, such as loading the tissue paper rolls and initiating the packaging process. They are ideal for small to medium-scale productions and offer a balance of automation and human control. Both types cater to different production requirements and offer unique benefits in tissue paper packaging.</p></p>
