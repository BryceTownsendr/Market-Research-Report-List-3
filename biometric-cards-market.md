<p><p>Biometric cards are categorized into two main types: contact biometric cards and contactless biometric cards. Contact biometric cards require physical contact with a card reader for authentication, while contactless biometric cards use radio frequency technology to transmit data wirelessly to the reader. Contact biometric cards offer higher security levels but may be less convenient to use, while contactless biometric cards provide faster and more convenient transactions, but may have lower security measures in place. Both types are gaining popularity in various industries for secure access control and payment systems.</p></p>
