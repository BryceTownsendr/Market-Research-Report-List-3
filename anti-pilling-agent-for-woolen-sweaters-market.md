<p><p>Anti-pilling agents for woolen sweaters are available in two main types: ionic type and nonionic type. Ionic anti-pilling agents work by forming ionic bonds with the wool fibers, reducing friction and preventing the formation of pills. On the other hand, nonionic anti-pilling agents work by creating a lubricating layer on the wool fibers, minimizing pill formation. Both types serve the same purpose of improving the appearance and durability of woolen sweaters, but they differ in their molecular interactions with the fabric.</p></p>
