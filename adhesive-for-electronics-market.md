<p><strong>The Adhesive for Electronics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Electrically Conductive</li><li>Electrically Conductive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive for Electronics market includes two main types, non-electrically conductive and electrically conductive. Non-electrically conductive adhesives are used for bonding components in electronics that do not require an electrical connection, providing mechanical strength and protection. Electrically conductive adhesives, on the other hand, are used for bonding components that require an electrical connection, allowing for the passage of current while also providing mechanical bonding. Both types play a crucial role in the assembly and performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
