<p><p>The appliance color-coated sheet market consists of two main types: PCM color-coated sheets and VCM color-coated sheets. PCM color-coated sheets are pre-painted metal sheets that provide durable and decorative finishes for household appliances. They are widely used in the manufacturing of refrigerators, washing machines, and air conditioners. On the other hand, VCM color-coated sheets are vinyl-coated metal sheets that offer enhanced scratch resistance and aesthetic appeal. They are commonly utilized in the production of kitchen appliances such as ovens, microwave ovens, and dishwashers. Both types of sheets play a crucial role in enhancing the overall aesthetics and longevity of household appliances.</p></p>
