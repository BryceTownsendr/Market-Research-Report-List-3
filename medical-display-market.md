<p><p>The medical display market comprises two major types of technology: LED and OLED. LED displays use light-emitting diodes to illuminate the screen, providing high brightness levels and excellent color accuracy. They are commonly used in medical imaging applications due to their reliability and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, OLED displays utilize organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, resulting in vibrant colors and superior contrast ratios. These displays are increasingly being adopted in medical devices and surgical displays due to their flexibility, thinner profile, and wider viewing angles.</p></p>
