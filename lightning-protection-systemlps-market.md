<p><p>Lightning Protection Systems (LPS) are designed to protect structures and equipment from the damaging effects of lightning strikes. Conventional LPS typically consist of lightning rods, conductors, and grounding systems. Non-conventional LPS, on the other hand, utilize technologies like early streamer emission air terminals and charge transfer systems. While conventional systems have been used for decades and are widely accepted, non-conventional systems offer advancements in lightning protection technology and are gaining popularity in the market. Ultimately, both types of LPS aim to provide effective protection against lightning strikes.</p></p>
